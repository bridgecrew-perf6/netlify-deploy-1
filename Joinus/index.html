Administration Specialist - Investment Research Division

 

Job Description:


 

A. Assist and cooperate with the research department of the investment bank to jointly develop investment concepts and provide portfolio consulting services for clients' wealth management.

B. Responsible for preparing related reports, presentations or proposals.

C. Work closely with account managers and analysts at all levels to improve customer satisfaction and business productivity and follow customer risk preferences.

D. Ensure that all transactions and trade bookings for the team are processed.

E. Provide quality customer service and ensure that all customer inquiries are handled and managed in a timely manner.

F. Coordinate team work and communication to ensure compliance with all regulatory requirements.

G. Work closely with account manager to execute orders.

H. Execute all types of investment transactions for clients accurately and efficiently.

I. Manage any temporary projects assigned.

 


Job Requirements:


 

1. University graduate, major in business or finance is preferred.

2. Have a good understanding of wealth management business and investment products.

3. Proficient in both spoken and written Chinese and English, fluent Mandarin is a must.

4. Enthusiastic, mature, careful, diligent, patient, independent and good at numbers.

5. Team player with good interpersonal and communication skills.

6. HKSI qualification, CFA, CPA or CFP is preferred.

7. Hard working and willing to learn.Strong desire to accept challenges.

8. Ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines.
