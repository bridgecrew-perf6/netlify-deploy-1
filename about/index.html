About

About

First, A proud track record


WEBULL is a company headquartered in Hong Kong, established in 1992, and currently has 8 branches in Korea, Singapore, the United Kingdom and the United States to maintain service continuity with customers and establish a good track record.


WEBULL is an investment advisory firm with a long history. Since the beginning of 2014, WEBULL has transformed into the world's largest professional cryptocurrency research advisory firm. It operates on a global scale and focuses on researching and trading hundreds of investment varieties (Forex, Gold, Cryptocurrency). It also provides financial and technical services to more than 547,000 cryptocurrency contracts, as well as retail customers and institutional investors.


It is this continuity of service and performance that has earned us a reputation for managing wealth between Europe, Asia, Africa, and the Americas, and cemented our relationships with larger institutions in all four regions. We strive to be the best in our field. With teams of experts across each of the eight divisions, we are sharp, efficient and aligned with our clients' values and expectations, adapting to the opportunities and challenges of trading across jurisdictions.


We focus on your needs and goals regarding expected returns, risks, and other considerations.

You will receive advice and personalized investment ideas about your overall portfolio and risk profile.

We have more than 6328 employees, including 716 analysts from around the world. Despite our leaner scale, we can provide high net worth private clients and institutional investors with a global perspective and award-winning expertise in both traditional and emerging investments. Our experts are always trying to analyze global economic and market trends. Our research on international financial markets, sectors and companies will help you make timely and informed investment decisions. We see wealth not only as an end in itself, but also as a means to enhance social and cultural values.


Second, Our Client Commitment


We put our clients at the centre of everything we do. Our advisors listen carefully to clients’ needs and are free to select the most suitable solutions to each client’s circumstances. With an entrepreneurial and nimble spirit, grounded by robust processes and rigorous research, we are committed to acting in our clients’ best interests and being with them every step of the way.


Third, Delivering the Best Outcomes


We are driven to do things the best way. If the best way involves challenging industry norms, or forging new links with expert partners, or going above and beyond – that’s what we do. We prize the entrepreneurial spirit, autonomy and agility that allow us to pursue opportunities to their conclusion and deliver high quality outcomes for our clients and advisors.


Fourth, Flexible, Full Service Capability


Being autonomous and not tied to any one service provider or financial institution means we work in the sole interests of our clients. While we have alliances to some of the most prominent financial institutions, our clients benefit from our personal perspective. This autonomy affords us the ability to act in our clients’ best interests – at the same time providing us the opportunity to tap into the best partners in our broader networks.


Fifth, We are Regional Experts


We continually strive to be the best in our field by bridging the opportunities between Europe and Asia. With expert teams on the ground in both regions, we are sharp, efficient, and aligned to our clients’ values and expectations; attuned to the opportunities and challenges in transacting between jurisdictions.


Sixth，Sustainable Investing


We take sustainable investing seriously because we believe that long-term sustainability issues can have a material impact on risk and return outcomes. An economically efficient, sustainable global financial system is a necessity for long-term value creation. Such a system will reward long-term, responsible investment and benefit the environment and society as a whole.


Seventh, Security is Paramount


We ensure our clients’ investments have a secure foundation. This means the investment platforms, products and management of these investments are accessed through large institutions that have extensive resources and industry strength. Our clients can have confidence knowing they are being looked after in every way.
